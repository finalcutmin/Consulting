The Virtual Development Office

An alternative approach to achieving your fundraising goals.

Our Virtual Development Office provides you with a full range of personalized fundraising services on an interim, transitional or on-going basis. We assess your capabilities, customize a plan and our hands-on team will hit the ground running and guide your organization to the next level of success.

  • A flexible and cost-effective solution to the traditional development office

  • Reduces the expense related to staff turnover
